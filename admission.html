Special Admission & Application for Enrollment

Special Selection

Selection Objectives

Students who align with the school's educational philosophy of "Broad Exploration, Freedom of Talent," possess self-directed learning and self-management skills, and are willing to challenge new educational models.

Selection Criteria
  1. Individuals with special expertise or professional skills.
  2. Individuals without special expertise but possessing basic self-directed learning abilities.
Selection Methods

A total of 32 students will be admitted (selected based on merit, with a certain number of reserve spots available). If there are any vacancies, they may be filled through the application channels.

A two-stage selection process will be implemented, consisting of the first stage of document review and the second stage of interviews.

Application for Enrollment.

Selection Objectives.

Students who possess the potential for "Deep Learning 6C Global Competencies" (Character, Citizenship, Communication, Collaboration, Critical Thinking, and Creativity) and demonstrate foundational academic performance.

Selection Criteria
  1. Eligibility Requirement: Students must have achieved a grade of A in any subject of the 113th Junior High School Education Examination in order to apply.
  2. Screening Criteria: Students who meet the eligibility requirement will be selected in the following order, with the number of candidates being twice the number of admission slots.
    1. Achieving an A++ in any subject.
    2. Achieving an A+ in any subject.
    3. Achieving an A in any subject.
  3. Excess Ranking: The ranking will be based on the total scores of the 113th Junior High School Education Examination in the Taipei North District. In the event of a tie in scores, those students will advance to the second stage of interviews.
Selection Methods

Admission Quota: 16 students + special selection return spots (selected based on merit, with a certain number of reserve spots available, and may admit fewer than the quota).

A two-stage selection process will be implemented, consisting of the first stage of evaluation and ranking, followed by the second stage of interviews.
